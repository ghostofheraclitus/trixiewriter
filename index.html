TrixieWriter 0.1a

Dear user, to the left is the field you type the story in. Everything you type is checked for Trixie-correctness that is to say, the code will check whether you use words outside the wordlist which is available on the right. The blue words are clickable and will open up a list of words that exist within their category. If you need to type a comment to the non-Trixie reader such as intended illustrations or anything of the sort, merely start a line with a # and nothing in it will be checked for Trixie-correctness. The line will also turn grey as a reminder. TrixieWriter ignores all other punctuation, so punctuate at will. Oh, and sorry for how buggy this is.
